# Task 1: Update `agents/` caller files to reference renamed review skills

Covers SDD `## Requirements` SR-3.1, SR-3.2, SR-3.3, SR-3.4.

## Context

First Task of Epic `t1.mak.su`. Epic 1 (`t1.mak.eo`) renamed the three review-skill directories and rewrote their `SKILL.md` frontmatter/body. After Epic 1 lands, four files under `agents/` still reference the OLD skill names — three reviewer-agent contracts and the PM role contract. This Task updates all four caller files in `agents/` to reference the new skill names.

The three reviewer-agent contracts (`code-reviewer.md`, `test-reviewer.md`, `doc-reviewer.md`) follow an identical structural pattern: a frontmatter `description:` line that names the corresponding wrapped skill, plus a body block that invokes the skill via the `Skill` tool, plus a closing line that defers to the wrapped skill's output. Each receives the same set of textual substitutions for its specific old-name → new-name pair. The PM role contract (`agents/pm.md`) is denser — six lines spread across the frontmatter `description:`, the static documentation about what the role does (allowlist rationale + responsibility statement), and the runtime in-flight orchestration prose with time-budget short-circuit guidance.

**Note on line numbers.** Line numbers cited in SDD SR-3 are illustrative as of SDD authoring and may have drifted by execution time. Use `grep` to locate references before editing — the `grep` AC checks at the bottom are the authoritative success signal regardless of line drift.

## What Needs to Change

Update four files under `agents/`. Per-file substitutions:

1. **`agents/code-reviewer.md`** — replace `bees-code-review` with `bees-engineer-review` everywhere it appears (lines 3 / 22 / 23 illustrative). The `Skill`-tool invocation must call `skill="bees-engineer-review"` after the substitution.
2. **`agents/test-reviewer.md`** — replace `bees-test-review` with `bees-test-writer-review` everywhere it appears (lines 3 / 22 / 23 illustrative). The `Skill`-tool invocation must call `skill="bees-test-writer-review"` after the substitution.
3. **`agents/doc-reviewer.md`** — replace `bees-doc-review` with `bees-doc-writer-review` everywhere it appears (lines 3 / 26 / 27 illustrative). The `Skill`-tool invocation must call `skill="bees-doc-writer-review"` after the substitution.
4. **`agents/pm.md`** — replace `bees-code-review` with `bees-engineer-review` and `bees-doc-review` with `bees-doc-writer-review` everywhere they appear (lines 3 / 8 / 19 / 202 / 203 / 204 illustrative). The PM dispatches `/bees-engineer-review` and `/bees-doc-review` (now `/bees-doc-writer-review`) inline during a per-Task review; references span the frontmatter `description`, the `Skill`-tool allowlist rationale, the responsibility statement, and the in-flight orchestration prose with time-budget short-circuit guidance.

For all four files, the substitutions are mechanical textual replacements. Do NOT modify the surrounding prose, the role's Instructions block, the role's filename, or any other content beyond the textual substitution of the old skill names.
