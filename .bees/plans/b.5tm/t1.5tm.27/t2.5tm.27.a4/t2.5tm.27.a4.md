## Context

The PM subagent definition is the most prose-heavy of the seven role files. It owns spec traceability against Grandparent-Bee egg-resolved docs (or the Bee body when egg is null/empty), cross-Task and cross-Epic interaction checks, time-budget short-circuit on review-skill loops, and orchestrates in-flight `/bees-code-review` and `/bees-doc-review` invocations during per-Task review. It also integrates the Scoped-marker discovery and scoping logic shipped by b.mu9/b.51d (post-commit `9c97dd1`) — both the quo-execute path (Grandparent-Bee marker check, hard-fail on helper exit 2) and the quo-fix-issue path (`up_dependencies`-based opportunistic discovery, best-effort fallback). The asymmetric error-handling between the two paths is intentional and load-bearing.

This Task is split into two subtasks: T3.S1 establishes the file with frontmatter and the lifted PM Instructions block (with a placeholder where the Scoped-marker prose goes); T3.S2 fills the placeholder with the dual marker-handling integration. T3.S2 depends on T3.S1.
